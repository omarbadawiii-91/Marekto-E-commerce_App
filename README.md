# market_app

Marketo is a modern, user-friendly e-commerce mobile app built for everyone who loves convenient, reliable online shopping. Whether you're hunting for the latest gadgets, authentic sportswear, must-read books, exciting video games, or exclusive curated collections — everything you need is right here in one beautiful app.

Key Features

Easy & Secure Sign-in
Get started in seconds — sign up or log in with email, Google, Apple, or continue as guest. Your data stays safe and private.
5 Curated Main Categories : 
• Sports — fitness equipment, sportswear, accessories
• Electronics — smartphones, laptops, gadgets, audio
• Collections — limited editions, vintage, exclusive drops
• Books — bestsellers, novels, comics, educational
• Games — consoles, PC games, accessories, merchandise

Powerful Market Search & Browse
Instantly find exactly what you want. Type a keyword, filter by category, price, rating — or just scroll through trending and recommended items. The smart search understands what you're looking for.
Rich Product Pages That Help You Decide

Tap any item and discover:
• Clear product name, current price & any discounts
• Average rating + number of reviews
• Detailed description, specifications, sizing info (when applicable)
• Real user comments & feedback — read what others think
• Add your own comment / review after purchase

Favorites – Never Lose Track of What You Love
Heart items you like → they instantly save to your personal Favorites list. Come back anytime to quickly re-check or buy later.
Your Personal Profile – Full Control

Profile : 
• Edit your name, profile picture, and preferences
• View complete purchase history
• Track orders (status, estimated delivery)
• Manage saved addresses & payment methods

Payment: 
Safe, Smooth Checkout & Payment
Multiple trusted payment options — complete your purchase with confidence. We prioritize security so you can focus on enjoying your new items.

Powered by Supabase
Marketo is built with modern tech for speed, reliability and scale:

Supabase handles secure authentication, fast database operations & real-time updates
Instant sync for products, prices, stock & orders
Built to grow — ready for thousands of happy shoppers
